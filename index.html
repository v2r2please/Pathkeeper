Pathkeeper is an app that turns small-scale acts of environmental restoration into a game!
Our team conceived of Pathkeeper to respond to our research which shows how everyday people desire to see a difference in the environmental health of our world.
Our future goals for Pathkeeper include collaborating with sponsors to create restoration project challenges.
